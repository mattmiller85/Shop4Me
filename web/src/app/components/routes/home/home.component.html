How does it work?

Shop4Me is a website that will allow you to create custom searches for various hard-to-find items. Once a search is configured and submitted, relevant results will be returned to the you for your review. You can then click a result to go to that site. Searches can be done live, or they can be saved and scheduled to run at a time you specify. If you do not instantly get a result, you can save that search, schedule it to run, and be notified once your search hits a result.
